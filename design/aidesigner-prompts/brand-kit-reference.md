## Tipografia
- **Sans:** Inter (títulos, labels, corpo)
- **Mono:** Inter Mono (IDs, timestamps, códigos, métricas)

## Mood
Technical, structured, dark mode. System UI aesthetic. High contrast. Monochromatic base with functional color accents.

## Anti-Patterns
- NO gradients
- NO neon/glow effects
- NO scanlines or grid backgrounds
- NO rounded "chat bubble" cards
- NO cartoon icons or illustrations
- NO sci-fi HUD elements
